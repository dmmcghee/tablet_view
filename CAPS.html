Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS)

Mental health and wellbeing is a priority in CAPS as they priotize individual counseling for individuals who are experiencing stresses of classes and everyday lives while also providing workshops and resources in which students can practice intential wellness. Sessions are offered both in person and veirtually via teletherapy. CAPS also have both scheduled and regular office hours but also 24/7 services that allows indivduals to form community to express themselves and gain guidance from one another. If interested if hearing more, check out the website.
