Maize and Blue Cupboard

The Maize and Blue Cupboard is a food and necessitites pantry located on central campus where students and staff who are food insecure and/or lacking proper aceessibility to a grocery store can get access to food, dining ware, personal and household supplies, and find access to support such as SNAP benefits. Need help and looking for information in regards to the pantry and there services or want to visit, please check out the website.
